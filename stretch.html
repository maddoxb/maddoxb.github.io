Stretch/Strength

My strength is my writing. I do feel like my strength has remained the same. However, I do feel like I have had one or two writing assignments that I feel did not showcase my strength to the best of my ability. Goal

I will modify my plan for the rest of the year to make it more organization oriented. My new goal is to keep my school binder/supplies organized for the rest of the year. This will improve my grades, and help my time management.
